What's the main concept?

"Entertain our visitors ---"

This is the deepest concept of this site. In the phase of fundamental designs, I researched a lot of extraordinary superior websites which other skillful enginner made with maniac enthusiasm; which made me overwhelmed and doomed.

I lost all my confidence and wanted to dissapear like a piece of chocolate in front of my wife when she is irritating. But I couldn't abandon my beloved wife whom I called from Brazil; the farthest place from Japan and my student loan which never-ending more than 15 years from now.

Therefore, I gave up to give up and get ready to compete the powerful opponents whom I once got frightened. You know it's a jungle fullfilled with super-size predetors. This little asian with mere 180cm height needs some strategy to survive. After 3000 times of reflections, I decided to bet on Entertaining; which I've used regularly on every crisis of mine in a quarter century.

Everytime when people laugh they can relax, open mind and be intrigued to the topic you speak. Besides it could be the most precious for happy life that how much you get entertained. So I always concerned about the point; can this choice really make my customers or members happier? I thought it is my nature and the best advantage to bet. This is the way. That's why I determined the main concept of this website, "Entertain our visitors ---".

I put all my passions to entertain you like examples of "Check it out" section below. I reccomend to go back again to the other page you've visited if you didn't notice my hidden thoughts. In the case you are intrigued by the creater, shall we get down to the business?

Technology

I used the libraries, frameworks or services below to implement this website. At least, I'm demonstrating you my abilities about them now.

Angular
A framework of screen. I chose it because I have experience of it at my business. At coding, I've strongly commited to the extendibility of contents. For example, I arranged all serifs into JSON files, which means I don't have to change code for enhance its contents. Sometime this kind of efforts would underestimate like just for convinience of developers, however I believe this influence of the growing speed on the service, since it makes less costs to release.
ngx-bootstrap
A CSS framework. Mainly I used it for responsive layout. I've tried to use its decorations but given up since it doesn't match the design concepts; Japanese horror taste. Hence I implemented the most of css decorations by myself.
anime.js
A Javascript animation library. Mainly I used it for complex animations. It was the first time I use, but I pretty like it since its basics are similar to normal css animation and easy to set up. I might use it for my next work as well.
RxJS
A JavaScript library for reactive programing. Check it out the official document about reactive programing. I used it to switch rooms in map. Just I wanted to use it for trial.
Flask
An API framework of Python. I used it for sending mail from contact form to my Gmail. It mediates as client between a user and Gmail API through OAuth2.0 Authorization Flow.
Gmail API
An API Service which supplied from Google. I thought it might apply for another automation at business or daily life.
