I lost all my confidence and wanted to disappear like a piece of chocolate in front of my wife when she is irritated. But I couldn't abandon my beloved wife whom I called from Brazil; the farthest place from Japan and my student loan which never-ending more than 15 years from now.

Therefore, I gave up to give up and get ready to compete with the powerful opponents whom I once got frightened. You know it's a jungle fullfilled with super-size predetors. This little Asian with mere 180cm height needs some strategy to survive.

To tell the truth, I was stuck. I came up with nothing on my mind. It is waste of time to continue thinking. Do you know what oriental people do the next in such cases? We try to learn from animals, like some styles of Kung Fu. Traditional biomimetics. That’s why I started to play Spider-Man.

That was a quite proper approach to find a resolution. Immitating a super hero, I remembered a precious point; he is also a “friendly-neighborhood”. It’s a must to have a great power to join as a reliable staff who complete a great responsibility. However Spider-Man is beloved for not only his might but also his cheerful witty personality that can overcome any vilans or poverty. Technology

I used the libraries, frameworks or services below to implement this website. At least, I'm demonstrating you my abilities about them now.

Angular
A framework of screen. I chose it because I have experience of it at my business. At coding, I've strongly commited to the extendibility of contents. For example, I arranged all serifs into JSON files, which means I don't have to change code for enhance its contents. Sometime this kind of efforts would underestimate like just for convinience of developers, however I believe this influence of the growing speed on the service, since it makes less costs to release.
ngx-bootstrap
A CSS framework. Mainly I used it for responsive layout. I've tried to use its decorations but given up since it doesn't match the design concepts; Japanese horror taste. Hence I implemented the most of css decorations by myself.
anime.js
A Javascript animation library. Mainly I used it for complex animations. It was the first time I use, but I pretty like it since its basics are similar to normal css animation and easy to set up.
AOS.js
A Javascript animation library. It can add appering effect easily. I recommend it because it can modernize your site quite easily.
RxJS
A JavaScript library for reactive programing. Check it out the official document about reactive programing. I used it to switch rooms in map, showing loading screen and so on.
Flask
An API framework of Python. I used it for sending mail from contact form. Of course I implemented Authentication, throttling or like that as basic manners of API security.
Send Grid
An Mail Sending Service which supplied from Twilio. You can send email without bothersome mail server setting through its Web API. I request it via the above-mentioned Flask API.
